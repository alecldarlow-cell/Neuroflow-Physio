Our specialist classes

How It Works

From your first enquiry to your first class

We take a careful, personalised approach to getting you into the right class. Every new participant begins with an individual assessment so Chloe can make sure the class is right for you — and that you feel confident and safe from day one.

Enquire

Initial Assessment (£65)

3

First Class — Free

Your first group session after assessment is complimentary. A chance to meet the group and experience the class before committing.

4

Book a Block

Book individual sessions or save with a block of 6. Blocks never expire and can be paused if your health requires it.

Flagship Class

Parkinson's Pilates

Our most specialised class — designed specifically for people with Parkinson's disease. Drawing on PD Warrior principles, LSVT BIG methodology and Pilates-based movement training, this high-amplitude class targets the specific motor challenges of Parkinson's: movement amplitude, rhythm, posture, balance and freezing. Motivating, challenging, and genuinely enjoyable.
