Who We Help

Specialist support for your condition

We run dedicated classes for specific neurological conditions — not generic exercise adapted to fit. Each class is built around the neuroscience of that condition.

🌀

Parkinson's Disease

High-amplitude movement classes drawing on PD Warrior and LSVT BIG principles. Targets posture, balance, movement speed and confidence. Strong evidence for slowing symptom progression.

🧩

Multiple Sclerosis

Gentle, paced functional strengthening designed to build capacity without triggering fatigue. Pilates-based core work that improves balance and quality of life in MS.

💫

Stroke Rehabilitation

Movement therapy that uses neuroplasticity — the brain's ability to rewire — to regain function and confidence. Continues the work of NHS physio long after discharge.

⚖️

Balance & Falls

Targeted reactive balance and strength training. Builds the confidence to move safely and independently in everyday life. Open to any neurological or age-related balance difficulty.

🧠

Acquired Brain Injury

Tailored rehabilitation programmes for people recovering from traumatic or acquired brain injury. Addresses coordination, strength, fatigue and functional movement.

🔄

Neuro Rehab Maintenance

For anyone who has completed NHS physiotherapy and wants to maintain and build on progress. Professional guidance and a supportive group — as long as you need it.
